## Our Story

LavaTech Solutions was founded in 2026 by a tech professional who saw a clear gap in Lava Hot Springs — there was simply no local option for technology services. Need a computer fixed? Drive to Pocatello. Starlink installed? Figure it out yourself. Want something 3D printed? Good luck.

That didn't sit right. Lava Hot Springs deserves the same access to tech services as any larger town. So LavaTech Solutions was born — a one-person operation dedicated to solving technology problems for local residents and businesses, right here in town.

## Our Mission

To make professional technology services accessible to everyone in Lava Hot Springs and the surrounding area — without the long drive, the long wait, or the impersonal experience.

## Our Values

**Local First**
We live and work in this community. When you call LavaTech, you're talking to your neighbor, not a call center.

**Honest & Transparent**
Every job gets a clear, upfront quote. If something isn't worth fixing, we'll tell you. We'd rather earn your trust than make a quick buck.

**Solve It Right**
We take the time to do things properly. A quick patch today that fails tomorrow isn't a solution — it's a future headache.

**Always Learning**
Technology evolves fast. We stay current so our community doesn't fall behind.
