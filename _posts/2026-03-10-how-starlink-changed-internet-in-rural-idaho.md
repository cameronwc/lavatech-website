Then Starlink arrived, and everything changed.

## The Before Times

Rural Idaho has always been underserved when it comes to internet connectivity. Many homes and businesses in the Lava Hot Springs area were stuck with download speeds under 10 Mbps — if they could get wired internet at all. For businesses trying to process credit card transactions, families with kids doing homework online, or anyone trying to stream a movie, it was a constant frustration.

## What Starlink Actually Delivers

We've installed Starlink for dozens of customers in the area, and here's what we consistently see:

- **Download speeds:** 50-200 Mbps (compared to 1-10 Mbps on old connections)
- **Upload speeds:** 10-20 Mbps
- **Latency:** 20-40ms (good enough for video calls and even gaming)
- **Reliability:** Dramatically better than legacy satellite, though weather can still cause brief interruptions

## The Installation Matters

Here's something people don't always realize: **where and how you mount the Starlink dish makes a huge difference**. The dish needs a clear view of the sky, and in a valley like Lava Hot Springs, that takes some planning.

We've seen customers lose 30-50% of their potential speed from bad dish placement. Trees, buildings, even the angle of a roof can create obstructions that cause dropouts.

That's exactly why we offer professional Starlink installation. We survey your property, find the optimal location, handle the mounting, and make sure your network is configured properly.
